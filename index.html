The fizzbuzz game

Fizzbuzz is a mathematical game for children to teach them about division.
 
Players sit in a circle and take turns to count from one, replacing any number divisible by 3 with &fizz, numbers divisible by 5 with "buzz," and numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 with "fizzbuzz."
